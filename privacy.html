Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Piano Spirits ("the App") is a freestyle piano app developed by PHAM VAN DAT. Your privacy matters deeply to us. This policy explains clearly what data the App handles and how.

1. Your Data

All recordings, settings, and preferences you create in the App are stored exclusively on your device. We have zero access to your content.

  • Piano recordings (video & audio)
  • App settings and preferences

2. Microphone & Audio

The App uses your device's microphone to record piano audio during screen recording. Audio is captured locally and stored on your device only — it is never uploaded or transmitted to any server.

3. Photos & Camera Roll

The App may request access to your Photo Library to save recorded videos. These videos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

4. Screen Recording

The App captures the piano stage view during recording sessions. Recordings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted externally.

5. Subscriptions & Purchases

The App may offer optional in-app subscriptions processed entirely through Apple's App Store via StoreKit. We do not collect or store your payment information — all transactions are handled by Apple. For details, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Analytics & Crash Reporting

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics (by Google) to collect anonymous usage statistics and crash diagnostics. This helps us understand how the App is used and fix problems faster.

  • Screen views and non-identifying events (e.g. recording started, language changed)
  • Crash reports with device model, OS version, and stack traces
  • No personally identifiable information is intentionally collected

Data handling is governed by Google's Firebase Privacy & Security.

7. Advertising

The App does not currently display ads and does not share data with advertising networks.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete any recording at any time from within the App. Deleting the App from your device permanently removes all locally stored data.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
